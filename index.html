Here's how SELAMAT helps you

Simple, private, and entirely at your own pace. No pressure, no fees. Ever.

1

You start

Tell us what happened

Fill in a short form or leave a voice recording if that feels easier. Share as little or as much as you're comfortable with. You don't even need to give your name.

Takes about 5 minutes
2

We review

We read every submission carefully

Within 2 to 3 working days, our team reviews your situation. We take the time to understand what you're going through. Not just the facts, but what you actually need.

Within 2 to 3 working days
3

We reach out

A gentle call or email from us

We'll contact you at the time that works for you, by phone or email. We'll talk through your situation and answer any questions you have. No pressure, no rush.

Your preferred time, your pace
4

We match

We find the right person for you

Based on your situation, we match you with the most relevant help. This could be a lawyer, doctor, counsellor, NGO, or support organisation. We explain who they are and why we think they're a good fit.

Lawyer · Doctor · NGO · Counsellor
5

Your choice

You decide. Always.

The options are yours to consider. You choose whether to proceed and when. We never push you toward anything. If the match doesn't feel right, we'll find another.

No obligation, no pressure
6

We introduce

We make the introduction for you

When you're ready, we handle the introduction so you don't have to explain everything from scratch. We bridge the gap so you can walk in with support already behind you.

We do the first step with you
7

Ongoing or handover

Stay with us or take it from here. Your call.

Tell us how involved you want us to be. We can stay alongside you until your case is closed. Or if you're ready to take it from here, we'll cheer you on from the sidelines. Either is completely fine.

Your comfort, your call

Zero fees. Zero hidden costs. Always.

SELAMAT exists for one reason only. To help you get the help you deserve. We will never charge you anything for any part of this process.
